Who we are

It all started down the hallway...

Brodsk Partners was founded by four INSEAD MBA graduates with the sole purpose of finding a single, profitable business to acquire and actively manage. Our expertise is backed by a dozen business professionals who are seasoned investors and successful entrepreneurs with extensive experience in sourcing deals, structuring transactions, and manage small and medium sized businesses.
